What is HP Pay?

HP Pay is mobile app that provides one stop payment solution for all your Fuel, LPG and Lubricant requirements of a customer along with exciting rewards in the form of Payback points. It’s Easy, Convenient and Quick.

How I can enroll for HP Pay ?

If you are a new customer, go to Google Play or App Store, type HP Pay and install the app in your mobile phone. Enter all the required information to Register for the Program. The Registration is Quick, Easy and Convenient.

I am HP Refuel Customer, do I have to download the HP Pay App Again ?

If you are an existing HP Refuel customer, you don’t need to register again for HP Pay. Once you access HP Refuel, it will guide you to app store/play store where you will have to use update option upon which HP Refuel will get upgraded to HP Pay. Your HP Refuel Profile and balances will be transferred to HP Pay automatically, you don’t need to register again. Also your Log in Credentials, Password and M-Pin will remain the same as that of HP Refuel and can be used for HP Pay.

How I can enroll for HP Pay ?

For existing HP Refuel customers , no need to Enroll again. If you are a new customer, go to your Google Play / App. Store, type HP Pay and install the app in your mobile phone and enter all the required information to Register / Enroll for the HP Pay Program. Login information will be sent to your registered mobile number. The Registration is Quick , Easy and Convenient.

Is HP Pay Available for all Mobile Handsets and Operating Systems?

HP Pay can be used on all handsets with iOS (version 9.0 & above) & Android OS (version 5.0 & above).

How can I add money to my wallet?

You can add money to your HP Pay Wallet by selecting the ‘Add Money’ option on your home screen.

  • There are four ways to Add money to your wallet viz.
    • Credit card
    • Debit card
    • Net Banking
    • UPI

      Once you enter the money and check the Terms and Conditions, you can select any of the above payment methods to load your wallet.

    • In case you wish to use Credit or Debit card, you have an option to save the card details that are entered by you so that you dont need to enter card details again and again for all future transactions.
    • In case of Net Banking, you can select the bank from which you wish to add money.
    • Once you enter details on your bank’s page & complete the transaction, you will be redirected to HP Pay with money added in your HP Pay wallet
      * Note : HPCL or their service providers will not ask for your Card Details , OTP / PIN or CVV . Kindly do not entertain any such calls. DO not share any of the details with any person.

My money has been debited from my bank account/credit card but it is not reflecting in Wallet, why?

Amount deducted from your bank/credit card is generally added to your HP Pay account instantly. However the entire transaction is dependent on connectivity at multiple interfaces and hence at times, the money doesn’t get added due to disruption in connectivity. In such rare scenario, if amount gets deducted from your bank account/credit card, the amount will get reversed to your account as per the timelines followed by banks. Generally, it takes about 3 to 5 working days depending on respective banks. We suggest you contact your bank’s customer care to know the time taken by their internal process.

Can I pay for fuel without loading my HP Pay Wallet ?

Yes, you can also make payment directly at the time of fuelling instead of loading the wallet in advance. You can use money available in your wallet for purchase of Petrol/Diesel in all our Retail outlets (Petrol Bunks) spread across the country. You can also use for making payment for your HP Gas cylinder and purchase of Lubricants.

What are the various methods for making payment ?

You can make payment by using either Paycode or QR code which are illustrated hereunder : Payment using Paycode: By selecting the ‘Pay by Paycode’ icon on your home screen, You can generate a 8 digit Paycode which has validity of 30 minutes. Once you visit the outlet, You can complete your fuelling and give the Paycode to pump attendant. Confirmation message will get displayed on the App page after the payment is successful. What is a BHIM UPI?

BHIM UPI (Unified Payment Interface) is a mobile payment mode that enables you to create a UPI ID linked to a bank account and then send or receive money using it. Additionally, you can also make payments to online merchants and outlets who accept BHIM UPI as a payment mode for transactions.

What is UPI ID or VPA?

UPI ID (also called Virtual Payment Address or VPA) is a unique identifier you can create in order to send and accept money via UPI. Once a UPI ID is created and mapped against your bank account, you can simply share your UPI ID (eg. abc@oksbi) with the payer. Once the payer enters your UPI ID and sends the money via UPI, it will be added to your account without having to enter any of your other bank details.

What is a UPI PIN?

UPI PIN is a 4 or 6 digit PIN that can be used for authenticating your UPI transactions. Your UPI PIN will be validated by the bank whose account you are using to pay with. The UPI PIN is different for different bank accounts.
